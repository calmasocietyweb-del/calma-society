A wedding in Menorca has something hard to match: the light, the sea behind, an estate among dry-stone walls. But behind any big day there's an invisible logistics, and transport is one of the pieces you notice most when it fails —and that no one remembers when it works—.

Handling it with elegance is simple: **a private chauffeur for the day**, with the right vehicle for each role. This service is operated by **[Menorca Bus](https://menorcabus.com)**, which beyond weddings also covers celebrations, events and groups across the island.

## Everyone in their place

- **The couple, in a Mercedes S-Class.** The arrival and departure of the big day, with the discretion and detail of quiet luxury.
- **The guests, in a Mercedes V-Class.** Comfortable journeys from the hotel to the estate and back, as a group, with no extra cars and no one hunting for parking at night.
- **The team and suppliers**, coordinated so everything arrives on time.

## Why it matters

Transport done well is what means **no one in the family has to play chauffeur**: everyone can enjoy themselves, raise a glass and get back calmly. With local drivers who know the estates, the lanes and the island's timings, the journeys stop being a worry and become part of the celebration.
