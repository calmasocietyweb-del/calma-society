The **Camí de Cavalls** (GR-223) is a historic path of about **185 km** that rings the whole island. Once used to watch the coast, today it's the best way to discover the Menorca you can't see from the car.

## How to walk it

It's split into **20 stages** of varying difficulty. You don't need to do it all — pick individual stretches by area and fitness.

- **For dream coves:** the **southern** stretches (Cala Galdana → Macarella, or towards Mitjana).
- **For wild scenery:** the **north** coast, rougher and more solitary.

## Tips

Bring **water, a hat and good shoes**; some stretches have little shade. Start early in summer and respect the surroundings — Menorca is a Biosphere Reserve. Walking it slowly is, precisely, the luxury.
