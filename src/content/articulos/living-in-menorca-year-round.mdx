Menorca seduces in August, but you truly understand it in **November**: when the coves empty, the towns find their rhythm again and the island becomes a place to **live**, not just visit.

## Life off-season

The Menorcan winter is mild and bright. There are markets, culture, long lunches and a community that knows itself. For those after real calm —the luxury money can't buy— it's the best time.

## A second home, done right

More and more people choose Menorca for a second home. A few principles we stand by:

- **Respect what makes the island unique:** stone, lime, human scale, landscape.
- **Think about all 365 days,** not just August.
- **Restore with judgement,** keeping the local character.
