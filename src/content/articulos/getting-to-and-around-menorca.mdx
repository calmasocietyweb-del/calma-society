## Getting there

- **By plane:** Menorca Airport (Maó) takes direct flights from mainland Spain and, in season, several European cities. The fastest option.
- **By ferry:** from Barcelona, Valencia and Palma (Mallorca). You can bring your car — handy for a longer stay.

## Getting around

- **Rental car:** the freest way to discover coves and towns. **Book ahead** in summer; demand is high.
- **Bus:** connects the main towns and, in season, several beaches (useful when car access to the coves is restricted).
- **On foot or by bike:** for stretches of the Camí de Cavalls and the historic centres.

> Tip: in July and August, many cove car parks fill by mid-morning. Start early or use the seasonal bus.
