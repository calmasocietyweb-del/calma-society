There's no single "best time" — it depends on what you want. Here's our take, calmly.

## By season

- **May and June:** our favourite. The sea is already pleasant, the light gorgeous, nature in bloom and **fewer people**. The island breathes.
- **July and August:** high summer, everything open and a festive mood (Sant Joan, Gràcia), but busier and pricier. Rise early for the coves.
- **September:** the secret. Warm water after summer, long days and a calm that returns.
- **Autumn and winter:** the authentic 365-day Menorca: food, culture, walks and low prices. Fewer services open, far more genuine calm.

> If you can choose, **June or September**: the best of the island without the crowds.
