Some trips start well from the very first minute. You step out of the terminal, someone is waiting with your name, opens the door of a black **Mercedes S-Class** and, in silence, the island begins to slide past the window. The fatigue of the flight is left behind. This is how Menorca should begin.

For decades the S-Class has been the benchmark saloon of **quiet luxury**: it doesn't shout, it whispers. It's the choice for those who value comfort without fuss, discretion and detail done right. In Menorca, this service is operated by **[Menorca Bus](https://menorcabus.com)** with local drivers.

## The perfect arrival

The first and last journeys are the ones you most appreciate having sorted. A transfer from **Menorca Airport (MAH)** to your accommodation in an S-Class turns that moment —usually the most stressful of the trip— into part of the experience: no taxi queues, no hunting for the rental car, no luggage to carry. Just step off the plane and let yourself be driven.

## Who it's for

- **Couples** after a serene, considered escape.
- **Executive travellers** and business trips, where punctuality and discretion are essential.
- **Special journeys:** dinner at the other end of the island, a celebration, an evening where you'd rather not think about the car or the way back.
- Anyone who simply understands that **the comfort of a journey is part of the luxury** of a trip.
