If Maó is the harbour and commerce, **Ciutadella** is history and elegance. It was Menorca's capital until the 18th century and keeps one of the prettiest old towns in the Mediterranean.

## Not to be missed

- **The old town**, of narrow streets and golden stone, with its **noble palaces** (some open to visit).
- **Menorca's Cathedral**, Gothic, raised over the former mosque.
- **Plaça des Born**, the city's monumental heart.
- **The harbour**, set in a long, narrow cove — perfect at dusk among restaurants and boats.

## When to go

Ciutadella is lovely year-round, but it shines during the **Festes de Sant Joan** (June), when horses take over the streets. Outside the big dates, a morning stroll is enough to fall for it.
