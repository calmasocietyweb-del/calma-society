When you travel as a family or a group, luxury takes a different shape: it's no longer just elegance, it's **space and comfort for everyone**. Being able to travel together, with plenty of room for luggage, parasols and the beach cooler, with no convoy of cars and no one stuck behind the wheel. That's what the **Mercedes V-Class** is for.

It's a spacious, comfortable vehicle, discreet and high-end, designed so a group can travel together and relaxed. In Menorca, this service is operated by **[Menorca Bus](https://menorcabus.com)** with local chauffeurs who know every lane on the island.

## The comfortable way to discover the island

The V-Class shines on **bespoke excursions**: a full day of south-coast coves, a route through the white villages, the northern lighthouses, the wineries, or a mix of it all. The chauffeur picks you up at your accommodation, suggests the best order to avoid the crowds and stops along the way wherever you like. You just enjoy the scenery.

And it solves exactly what's most awkward in summer: reaching **coves with restricted access**, or car parks that fill at dawn, without setting an alarm to fight for a space.

## Who it's for

- **Families** with children, car seats, luggage and everything a beach day demands.
- **Groups of friends** who want to travel together and at their own pace.
- **Airport transfers** when arriving as a group and with suitcases.
- Any plan where comfort and space make the difference: weddings, celebrations or a full island day without driving.
